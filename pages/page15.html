GLPI Plugin

Our GLPI external plugin provides seamless integration with LINE, a popular messaging platform, facilitating enhanced communication and collaboration within asset management and IT service processes. This plugin serves as a conduit for bidirectional data exchange, enabling users to receive real-time notifications, alerts, and updates directly through their LINE accounts. By leveraging this integration, organizations can optimize their workflows, improve responsiveness, and foster greater efficiency in managing assets and resolving IT issues. With our plugin, teams can streamline communication channels, expedite decision-making, and deliver superior service quality, ultimately enhancing productivity and satisfaction across the board.
