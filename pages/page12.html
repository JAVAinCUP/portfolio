Call tracking System(CTS) and CRM

Our call tracking system seamlessly integrates with CRM and inventory management solutions, offering a comprehensive platform for businesses to manage customer interactions and inventory levels efficiently. By capturing and analyzing call data, including call duration, source, and outcome, our system enriches CRM records with valuable insights, enabling sales and support teams to personalize customer interactions and improve service quality. Additionally, our integration with inventory management allows for real-time synchronization of product availability and order status.
