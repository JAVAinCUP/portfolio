Line intregting

Through this integration, users can receive notifications, alerts, and updates directly within their LINE accounts, ensuring real-time access to critical information related to asset management, ticketing, and IT service management processes. The plugin supports bi-directional communication, enabling users to interact with GLPI functionalities directly from their LINE interface, such as submitting support requests, updating ticket statuses, or accessing asset information.
