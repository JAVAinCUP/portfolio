POS mobile application

Our university software project focused on developing a Point of Sale (POS) system tailored for mobile devices. The POS application aimed to streamline transactions and inventory management for small businesses by providing a user-friendly interface accessible via smartphones or tablets. Through innovative features such as real-time sales tracking and inventory updates, our solution aimed to enhance operational efficiency and customer service for retailers on-the-go.

Technologies

  • React-native
  • Nodejs
  • Express
