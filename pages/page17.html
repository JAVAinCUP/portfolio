Car detection

Our project revolves around the development of a car detection system aimed at detecting and addressing breaking traffic rules. Leveraging advanced computer vision algorithms and machine learning techniques, our system identifies vehicles within a given traffic scene and analyzes their behavior in real-time. Through the integration of cameras or sensors at strategic locations, our solution monitors illegal lane changes. The system alerts authorities or relevant stakeholders instantly upon detecting a violation, facilitating swift intervention and enforcement of traffic regulations.
